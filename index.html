About Me

I'm a Full-Stack Developer based in São Paulo, Brazil.

I've been working in the IT industry for 6 years. Started with my own lan house when I was 18 and later moved into operational/infrastructure IT enviroment as analyst at small and medium-sized enterprises based in São Paulo. Because of that, I got ceritified in ITIL® 2011 and ISO 20K for client side requiriment.

I decided to move from the IT ops to development to be part of the technological innovation of the business. This also matches my personal lifestyle, which is working through a wifi hotspot with my VPN, avoiding the chaotic traffic of the city that I like to live in and without having to worry about the unforeseen circumstances caused by it.

Success is my only option as freelancer, failure is not. So to do that challenge, I've been keep updated with new technologies and study a lot to catch up all my goals.

I make sure my designs meet industry standards and design in a mobile-first aproach.

Design Process



DISCOVER
Research is done in order to know the client’s business better and understand what they really need. All the parties are aligned with the objectives of the project.


PLAN
Ideas and details are chalked out on how the project will proceed. It includes ploting content and marketing strategies in order to achieve the client’s goals.


DEVELOP
Ideas are brought into reality and Quality Assurance Testing is performed to verify that the site correctly functions and is aligned with the design specifications.


DELIVER
The product is delivered to the client and post implementation support is offered (if needed). The project is now considered complete as the clients requirements are met.
